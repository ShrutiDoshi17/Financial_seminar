What Is FinSeminar?

FinSeminar is a role-based financial education management platform that eliminates the chaos of managing financial workshops manually. We bring institutions, professionals, and participants onto one secure, unified system.

Built with Angular and Spring Boot, secured with JWT authentication — every user sees only what they need, exactly when they need it.
